Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · DEWEB (dewebam.com)

DEWEB (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the dewebam.com platform — an IT studio and marketplace where clients order services and sellers offer products using DEWEB internal coins.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: username, email, password (stored encrypted), profile and seller information.
  • Transaction data: orders, wallet balance, DEWEB transfers, and crypto top-up references.
  • Communications: contact form messages, support chat, and service inquiries.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and cookies (see Cookie Policy below).

2. How we use your information

  • Provide and secure your account and payments.
  • Process orders between customers and sellers.
  • Send support responses and optional newsletters (if you opt in).
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

3. Sharing of data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with payment/swap partners only as needed to complete crypto top-ups or withdrawals, and with service providers that host our infrastructure under confidentiality obligations.

4. Security

We use HTTPS, encrypted passwords, and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we work to protect your data using industry-standard practices.

5. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account data by contacting us through the site support widget or contact form. You may withdraw marketing consent at any time in your account settings.

6. Retention

We retain account and transaction records as long as your account is active and as required for legal, tax, or dispute resolution purposes.

Terms of Use

  • You must be at least 18 years old to use DEWEB.
  • DEWEB coins are internal platform credits (1 DEWEB = 1 USD conceptually), not a public cryptocurrency unless stated otherwise.
  • Sellers must deliver agreed work; buyers pay in DEWEB per platform rules.
  • Prohibited: fraud, illegal content, abuse of other users, or circumventing platform fees.
  • We may suspend accounts that violate these terms.
  • The platform is provided “as is”; liability is limited to the extent permitted by applicable law.

Cookie Policy

Required cookies (necessary to use the site):

  • Authentication session (login token).
  • Cookie consent preference.
  • Security and load-balancing.

Optional cookies (if you choose “Accept all”):

  • Language preference.
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve the product.

If you decline cookies, the site cannot function and you will not be able to register or sign in.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy: use the support chat on dewebam.com or the Contact section on the home page.
